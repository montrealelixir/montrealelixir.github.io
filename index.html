About Our Community

Montreal Elixir is more than just a meetup group – we're a thriving community of developers, engineers, and enthusiasts passionate about the Elixir ecosystem. Whether you're building web applications with Phoenix LiveView or exploring OTP patterns, our community welcomes developers of all experience levels.

Join us for monthly talks we dive deep into the world of concurrent, fault-tolerant applications powered by the BEAM virtual machine.

What We Offer

Monthly Meetups

Join us every last Wednesday of the month to learn and share your passion for the Elixir programming language and the BEAM virtual machine.

Community Discussion

Connect with fellow Elixir enthusiasts on Discord where you can ask questions, share knowledge, and discuss the latest in the Elixir ecosystem.

Past Talks

Watch recordings of our previous meetups and presentations on YouTube to catch up on the latest discussions and learn from community members.
